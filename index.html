Stage 3 Deep Dive

Preprocessing Stack

1

Resize → 384×384

Standardizes all input frames to a consistent square resolution for uniform flow computation.

2

Grayscale Conversion

BGR to single-channel gray — reduces dimensionality while preserving structural information.

3

NLM Denoising

fastNlMeansDenoising(h=10, templateSize=7, searchSize=21) — removes Gaussian and impulse noise.

4

CLAHE Enhancement

clipLimit=2.5, tileGrid=(8,8) — adaptive histogram equalization avoids over-amplifying noise in flat regions.

5

Bilateral Filter

d=7, σColor=50, σSpace=50 — edge-preserving smoothing that removes texture while keeping boundaries sharp.

6

Laplacian Sharpening

Custom 3×3 kernel [[0,-1,0],[-1,5,-1],[0,-1,0]] — enhances fine structural edges for better flow estimation.

Quality Metrics

Accuracy Thresholds

🌊

Optical Flow Magnitude

threshold = median + 1.2 × σ

90th-percentile magnitude of Farneback dense flow field flags frames with large-scale spatial displacement.

82% weight in detection
🔁

SSIM Dissimilarity

threshold = mean + 0.8 × σ

Structural similarity loss (1 − SSIM) captures perceptual changes between consecutive frames beyond pixel differences.

68% contribution to dual gate
📡

PSNR Score

PSNR = 20 log₁₀(255 / √MSE)

Signal-to-noise ratio in dB. Frames above 25 dB with SSIM > 0.75 are counted as successfully corrected.

Pass threshold: 25 dB
✅

Final Accuracy

accuracy = improved / total × 100

Percentage of frames where both SSIM and PSNR exceed their thresholds after motion correction is applied.

Target: > 90%
